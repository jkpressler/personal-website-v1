BIO

I am a 24 year old Japanese-English billingual, currently living and working in Tokyo. I work as a project engineer in the automotive industry during the day, then practice and learn various programming topics at night. Growing up between the US and Japan, I have been lucky enough to get a strong understanding of both cultures and languages. Since moving to the US from Okinawa at about 6 years old, I have lived mostly in the US, while returning for summers in Japan every third year or so.

Education: BSc Electrical Engineering w/ Math Minor from The University of Nebraska-Lincoln
